    Discover Andean Delights: A Culinary Journey in Atlanta

    At Andes Gastronomía, we invite you on a culinary journey to the heights of Ecuadorian cuisine, right here in Atlanta. Nestled in the heart of the city, our restaurant brings the vibrant flavors and rich cultural heritage of Cuenca to your table.

    Each dish is carefully crafted to showcase the authentic essence of Andean cooking, offering a taste of Ecuador's culinary traditions in every bite.

    • Authentic Ecuadorian Flavors: Experience the vibrant tastes of Cuenca, Ecuador, with our menu designed to capture the essence of Andean cuisine.
    • Locally Sourced Ingredients: We pride ourselves on using the freshest local ingredients, ensuring each dish reflects our commitment to quality and sustainability.
    • Culinary Excellence: Our skilled chefs blend tradition and innovation to create unforgettable dining experiences, where every dish is a celebration of Ecuadorian culture and culinary craftsmanship.

    Join us at Andes Gastronomía for a dining experience that transports you to the cobblestone streets and bustling markets of Cuenca, Ecuador, with every mouthwatering bite.

    Why choose Our Restaurant

    Andes Gastronomía in Atlanta brings the vibrant flavors of Cuenca, Ecuador, to your table. From tangy ceviches to hearty cazuelas, our menu offers a taste of Andean culinary traditions. With locally sourced ingredients and a commitment to excellence, we invite you to savor the essence of Ecuadorian culture in every bite.

    Pescado Encocado

    Indulge in Coastal Bliss: Where Coconut Meets Fresh Catch.

    Succulent fish fillet simmered in a creamy coconut milk sauce, infused with the vibrant flavors of bell peppers, onions, and cilantro. Served with fluffy rice for a delightful tropical feast.

    Lomo Saltado

    A Stir-Fry Sensation Straight from the Andean Highlands.

    Tender beef tenderloin strips stir-fried with onions and tomatoes in a savory soy sauce and vinegar marinade. Served with crispy French fries and rice for a satisfying taste of Ecuadorian-Chinese fusion.

    Arroz Marinero

    Dive into Oceanic Delight: Seafood Symphony in Every Bite.

    A tantalizing blend of mixed seafood including shrimp, fish, calamari, and mussels, nestled in fragrant rice infused with bell peppers, onions, and garlic, all enveloped in a rich and savory tomato sauce.

    Cazuela de Mariscos

    A Warm Embrace of the Sea: Comfort in Every Sip.

    A comforting seafood medley featuring shrimp, fish, calamari, and mussels, simmered to perfection with hearty yuca (cassava), sweet corn, bell peppers, and onions in a flavorful tomato broth.

    Churrasco con Chimichurri

    Grilled to Perfection: Flavor Explosion Awaits with Every Bite.

    Grilled beef steak marinated to juicy perfection, served with a zesty chimichurri sauce made with fresh parsley, garlic, vinegar, and olive oil. Accompanied by creamy mashed potatoes and grilled vegetables for a satisfying and flavorful meal.
